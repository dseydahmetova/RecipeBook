French Toast

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
bread 6 slices
egss 2
milk 2/3 cup
salt 1/4 table spoon

Step 1 Beat together egg, salt, milk.

Step 2 Heat a lightly oiled skillet over mediium-high heat.

Step 3 Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides.

Step 4 Place in pan and cook on both sides, untill golden.

Baked chicken breast

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
chicken breast 2
olive oil 1/4 cup
garlic 1/2 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
pepper 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1 Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil and garlic, sprinkle salt and pepper. Arrange chicken in a large baking dish and refrigerate at least 45 minutes.

Step 2 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Step 3 Bake in preheated oven until chicken meat is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 45 to 60 minutes.

Step 4 Place in pan and cook on both sides, untill golden.

Lentil soup

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
onion 1
olive oil 1/4 cup
garlic 2 cloves
carrot 2
celery 2 stalks
crushed tomato 1 can
lentil 2 cups
vinegar 2 tablespoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
pepper 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1 In a large soup pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery; cook and stir until onion is tender. Stir in garlic, cook for 2 minutes.

Step 2 Stir in lentils, and add water and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer for at least 1 hour. Stir in vinegar, and season to taste with salt and pepper, and more vinegar if desired.
